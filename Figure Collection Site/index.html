Welcome to DeliriousB's Figures and Merch Blog!

This blog is dedicated to my figures, plushies, and other merchandise. Over the years I've amassed a substantial amount of merchandise from video games, anime, manga, and original works. This site is a way for me to display them to the world as well as provide reviews for merchandise which I myself own and can give personal input on. I hope you enjoy my collection and benefit from my reviews.


Hit the Collection tab to go to image sets of different collections I have.


Hit the Reviews tab to see and read reviews I have on figures or other merchandise I own which I give my two cents on.


Hit the Donate tab if you're feeling generous and would like to Paypal me. Buying all this swag isn't cheap even though the Japanese Yen is usually weaker than the dollar by a bit.


Hit the Links tab for links I consider useful to other collectors. These are sites which I may buy some of my merchandise from or other sites I consider beneficial to collectors.
