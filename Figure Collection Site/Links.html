Links

Here's some links that I think other collectors will find useful. Generally speaking, these are places I shop online for figures and other various collectables.


AmiAmi is definitely one of the best sites out there you can preorder figures and other miscellaneous merchandise like character umbrellas from straight from Japan. They offer practically the lowest possible price you can find for figure preorders and they are extremely reliable.


http://www.amiami.com/


GoodSMILE online store is preordering figures straight from the source. They are more expensive that AmiAmi, but on some items they have preorder bonuses like extra props that I believe are worth it and sometimes they have web exclusive items that you can't buy from other online vendors.


http://goodsmileshop.com/en/


HobbyLink Japan is another good site to shop for figures. They are slightly cheaper than GoodSMILE but far more expensive than AmiAmi in some categories. However, unlike AmiAmi they keep a far larger stock of their items, so usually if a figure I want is no longer available on AmiAmi I use HobbyLink Japan as a back up plan.


http://hlj.com
